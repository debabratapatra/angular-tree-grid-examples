Specific Row Expand/Collapse

Sometimes we may need to expand or Collapse a specific Row in the grid programatically. We can do that by using below methods.

Expand

    {{"expandRow(id) {"}}
    {{"   this.angularGrid.expandRow(id);"}}
    {{"}"}}

Collapse

    {{"collapseRow(id) {"}}
    {{"   this.angularGrid.collapseRow(id);"}}
    {{"}"}}
